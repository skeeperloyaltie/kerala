Cardiology

Expert care for your heart with advanced technology and compassionate specialists.

Our Cardiology Department offers comprehensive heart care, from diagnostics like ECG and echocardiography to advanced treatments such as angioplasty. We prioritize your cardiovascular health with personalized plans and state-of-the-art facilities.

Neurology

Specialized care for brain and nerve conditions with cutting-edge solutions.

Our Neurology Department provides expert diagnosis and treatment for conditions like stroke, epilepsy, and Parkinson’s disease. Using advanced imaging and a multidisciplinary approach, we ensure the best outcomes for your neurological health.

Hepatology

Dedicated liver care with expertise and precision.

Our Hepatology Department focuses on liver diseases such as hepatitis and cirrhosis. With advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans, we support your liver health and overall well-being.

Pediatrics

Gentle, expert care for your little ones.

Our Pediatrics Department offers comprehensive healthcare for children, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments. We create a comforting environment to ensure your child’s health and happiness.

Ophthalmology

Advanced eye care for clear vision and healthy eyes.

Our Ophthalmology Department provides expert care for eye conditions, from routine exams to complex surgeries like cataract removal. We use the latest technology to protect and enhance your vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about your care at CityHospital.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our website’s "Appointment" section or by calling our reception at +91 80 4120 5678. Simply provide your details, choose a department, and select a doctor and time that suits you.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, any previous medical records, and your insurance details if applicable. For new patients, arriving 15 minutes early helps complete registration smoothly.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please contact our billing department or check with your provider to confirm coverage before your visit.

What are your emergency services like?

Our Emergency Department operates 24/7 with a skilled team ready to handle urgent cases. We prioritize rapid response and comprehensive care to stabilize and treat patients effectively.

Can I get a second opinion?

Absolutely. We encourage patients to seek second opinions. Our specialists are happy to review your case and provide expert insights to support your healthcare decisions.

How do I access my medical records?

You can request your medical records by contacting our reception or visiting our patient portal online. We ensure your information is secure and accessible when you need it.
